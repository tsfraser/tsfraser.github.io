About me:

Prior to starting my PhD at UWaterloo, I did my MSc at the University of St Andrews, with my Master's thesis focusing on applying unsupervised clustering to galaxies from the IllustrisTNG-100 simulation. Our work led to some very interesting results, which have currently been submitted as a publication to MNRAS (Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society: LINK HERE), and as a poster with preliminary results to the 2021 National Astronomy Meeting (NAM2021 poster) . I did my undergraduate studies at the University of Toronto, with an Honours BSc degree specialising in physics with a minor in mathematics.
